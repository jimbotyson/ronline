R: Testing hypotheses

In this topic we will look at the procedures for hypothesis testing in R.Â  Hypothesis testing is the procedure whereby we accept or reject some assertion about a population parameter based on inference from facts about a sample (since information about the whole population is often not available).

Consider this question: are men and womens' salaries in the UK equal (on average)?

We would say that the null hypothesis is that they are equal - or equivalently that there is no relationship between gender and salary.

The hypothesis test assesses the statistical evidence against this hypothesis to see if it is so strong that it should be rejected.

In what follows we will look at a number of tests:

  • the family of t-tests
  • the ANOVA
  • the Chi-square test
  • correlation
  • regression modelling
