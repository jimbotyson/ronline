Second task

If a household has children and the household income is less than 100000 (that is to say, it is in the first quartile for the variable FINCP), we want to classify that household as having children in poverty.

To do this you should create a binary factor variable with values 0/1 and assign it appropriate values for the cases of the data selected in the first task.

What is the mean household income of those households having children in poverty?
What proportion of households overall own a laptop?
What proportion of households now identified as having children in poverty own a laptop?
